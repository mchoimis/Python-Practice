Minjung Choi

PhD student, MIS

Minjung is a Presidential Fellow and PhD student at Temple University's Fox School of Business (MIS). She obtained a MS and a BBA from Seoul National University, with a one-year exchange in HEC Paris.

She tries to understand online phenomena from a network perspective. Most recently, she is interested in (i) how networks of individuals and/or entities can help better predict user content generation and opinion formation, and (ii) how location-aware mobile apps change the way users engage. She also studies (iii) how navigational search engines affect online product demands.

In her spare time, she enjoys non-academic writing. As a programmer and civic innovator at heart, she's a member of Code for America Philadelphia.
