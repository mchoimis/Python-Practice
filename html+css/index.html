Minjung Choi

PhD student, MIS

Minjung is a Presidential Fellow and PhD student at Temple University's Fox School of Business. Before joining the doctoral program in 2012, she obtained a MS and a BBA from Seoul National University.

She tries to understand online phenomena from a network perspective. She is interested in how networks of individuals and/or entities on the web can help better predict or infer about online communities, opinion formation, and content generation. Her other research interests include how online search engines especially navigational meta-search websites affect product demands, and how mobile apps transform the way users engage.

In her spare time, Minjung enjoys non-academic writing. As a programmer and civic innovator at heart, she's also a member of Code for America Philadelphia.
