Current Research Projects

1. "Mobile Social Networks and User Engagement"

We propose a method for customer segmentation using behavioral data on a location-aware mobile app.
* Keywords: mobile, geospatial, social networks, classification problem
* w/ Sunil Wattal

2. "Hidden Affinity Networks in Online Communities" - developed from class in Fall 2013

We construct a matrix for better prediction of user opinion in online communities.
* Keywords: online communities, social networks, opinion influence, network inference
* w/ Steven L. Johnson, Youngjin Yoo

3. "Navigational Search and Demand Adjustment" - 2nd year paper

We hypothesize navigational search engines (e.g., travel deal search) will impact customer's evaluation of product price and demand.
* Keywords: online search, metasearch engines, price adjustment, product display
* w/ Sunil Wattal

4. "Temporal and Geospatial Anchors in Customer Ratings" - developed from class in Fall 2013

We find anchoring in human judgment exists in local business ratings. We study the conditions in which it will have impacts.
* Keywords: anchoring effects, customer rating
* w/ Sangsuk Yoon, Nathan Fong
